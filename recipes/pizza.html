Pizza

Pizza is the best dish in the world, originated from some genius mastermind in Italy

Ingredients

  • Dry yeast
  • Water
  • Flour
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mix flower with water
  2. Add yeast
  3. Add olive oil
  4. Mix until fully combined
  5. Let it rest until it doubles in size
  6. Spread dough in a flat circle
  7. Add toppings
  8. Bake with the heat of a thousand suns
